Academic Publications

New Features for the Detection of Fetal QRS Complexes in Non-Invasive Fetal Electrocardiograms
Annual International Conference of the IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBC), 2023

Proposed a computationally efficient method for detecting fetal QRS complexes from single channel non invasive ECG signals without maternal ECG cancellation. The approach leverages spectral differences between maternal and fetal components and combines time and time frequency features with k means clustering.
Validated on real and synthetic public datasets, achieving up to 96.3 percent sensitivity and 90.4 percent F1 score, demonstrating accurate and low complexity fetal heart rate monitoring.

Deep Learning with Sparse Representation for Biomedical Signals
IEEE, 2023

Developed two frameworks that integrate wavelet based multiresolution analysis into deep convolutional neural networks to improve model interpretability and efficiency.
The first framework combines continuous wavelet transform with DCNN for fetal ECG identification from non invasive recordings. The second embeds discrete wavelet transform filter banks within an encoder decoder architecture for brain MRI skull stripping and tissue segmentation.
The unified approach reduces model complexity while maintaining strong performance across biomedical signal and imaging tasks.
